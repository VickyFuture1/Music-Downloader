Facts & Solutions

I don't find search queries

If you're having trouble finding search queries, try the following:

  • Refresh the website
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try searching again with different keywords
  • Check your internet connection

If the issue persists, please contact us at mp3contactus@gmail.com.

How can I search trending songs?

To find trending songs:

  • Scroll down to the Trending Songs section on the homepage
  • Browse through the recommended songs, sorted by views and likes on YouTube
  • Click on the desired song thumbnail to search for it automatically
  • The song will appear in the search results, where you can download it
  • Use the "Refresh" button to load more trending songs

What file formats are supported?

Our website allows you to download content in the following formats:

  • MP3 - Audio format (available for both YouTube and SoundCloud)
  • MP4 - Video format (YouTube only)

Note: Some content may have restrictions based on the source platform's policies.

The play function is not working

If you're experiencing issues with the play function:

  • Some videos or songs may not be available in your region due to copyright restrictions
  • Try searching for an alternative version of the song or video
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check if your browser supports HTML5 video playback
  • Try refreshing the page or using a different browser

If the problem continues, please contact us at mp3contactus@gmail.com with details about the issue.

Download speed is slow

If downloads are taking longer than expected:

  • Check your internet connection speed
  • Try again during non-peak hours
  • Close other bandwidth-intensive applications
  • Consider using a wired connection instead of WiFi if possible

Other issues

For any other problems not listed here:

  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Disable any ad-blockers or extensions that might interfere
  • Try accessing the site from a different device

Our support team is always ready to help at mp3contactus@gmail.com.
